Ingredients

  • 250g minced beef
  • 120g / 4 slices of tasty cheddar
  • 1 bun with sesame seeds
  • 1 onion
  • 100g / 2 slices of bacon

Steps

  1. Cut onion on circles
  2. Bake buns for 5 mins to get crusty
  3. Throw that piece of meat on a grill (or pan)
  4. Add bacon to the grill
  5. Throw that cheddar on top of it once it's done from one side
  6. Smear mayo and ketchup on a bun
  7. Carefuly place beef with cheddar, then onion on top
  8. Cover with other half of the bun
