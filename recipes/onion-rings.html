Ingredients

  • 4 onions
  • breadcrumbs
  • 4 eggs
  • flour

Steps

  1. Cut onion on circles
  2. Wrap in eggs and flour
  3. Throw into a heated oil
  4. Let it fry
  5. Take out and put on a kitchen towel
  6. Serve
